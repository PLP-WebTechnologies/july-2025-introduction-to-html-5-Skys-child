Why Use Semantic HTML?

Semantic HTML helps search engines and assistive technologies understand your content better. Elements like <header>, <nav>, <main>, and <footer> add meaningful structure.

Accessibility Best Practices

Using landmarks, headings in logical order, and attributes like aria-label improves navigation for users with screen readers.

Quick Tip

Always provide descriptive text for links and ensure your headings follow a proper hierarchy for clarity and SEO.
